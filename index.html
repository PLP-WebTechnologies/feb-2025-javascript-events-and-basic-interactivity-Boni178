Classic Margherita Pizza

Servings: 2 pizzas | Prep Time: 20 minutes + rising | Cook Time: 10-12 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups (300g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet) instant yeast
  • 1 cup (240ml) warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup (240ml) pizza sauce
  • 8 oz (225g) fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil, for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, salt, sugar, and instant yeast.
  2. Add the warm water and olive oil. Stir until a dough forms.
  3. Knead on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let rise in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 500°F (260°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.
  6. Divide the dough into two portions. Roll out each to your desired thickness.
  7. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza peel. Spread with pizza sauce, top with mozzarella slices, and add fresh basil leaves.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil before serving. Enjoy!

Tiramisu

Servings: 6 | Prep Time: 30 minutes + chilling

Ingredients:

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup (160ml) milk
  • 1 1/4 cups (300ml) heavy cream
  • 8 oz (225g) mascarpone cheese
  • 24 ladyfinger cookies
  • 1 cup (240ml) strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder, for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until well blended. Whisk in milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils. Boil gently for 1 minute, then remove from heat and allow to cool slightly. Cover and chill in the fridge for 1 hour.
  2. In a medium bowl, beat cream until stiff peaks form.
  3. Whisk mascarpone into the chilled yolk mixture until smooth.
  4. In a small bowl, combine coffee and coffee liqueur (if using). Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture (do not soak).
  5. Arrange half of the soaked ladyfingers in the bottom of a 9x9 inch dish. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers, then half of the whipped cream. Repeat layers.
  6. Cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours or overnight. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.
